Frank Ocean

Bio

Frank Ocean burst on to the music scene with his debut mixtape, Nostalgia ULTRA. It was released on February 16, 2011. The album was planned to be released as an EP but was scrapped. Only two songs made were released: "Swim Good" and "Novacane" He found the most commercial success after releasing Channel Orange (2012) and Blonde (2016). His fans are still waiting for his newest album. Ocean has released nine singles since his latest album hitting another is soon to come.
